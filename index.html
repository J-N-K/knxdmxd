What is knxdmxd?

knxdmxd is a daemon that sends E1.31-data (DMX over Ethernet, similar to Artnet) and is controlled by KNX/EIB telegrams using eibd. So basically you need a device that understands E1.31 (e.g a "bridge" to standard DMX/E1.11) and a working eibd-setup.
